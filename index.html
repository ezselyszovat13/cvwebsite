WHO AM I?

The biggest achievement goes to my parents, who made it possible to get a first name which is part of my hometown. Therefore I just became Szovát from Bágyogszovát.

The connection between me and my profession has started at secondary school where I learned C# as my main language. I also participated in some contests to achieve confidence for the years to come. After my fifth year, I acquired my trade, nonetheless, I did not finish my studies here.

I was admitted to ELTE, where I was learning Computer Science at these times. I picked up comprehensive knowledge here in various subjects, although I consider C++ and PHP (Laravel) as my most valuable picks. At the start, I only cared about my studies to achieve, though for a semester I was giving weekly lectures and supervising exam tasks for the subject Object-Oriented programming in C++ in ELTE.

In the last year of my BSc, I joined Leviathan Solutions, where I could be part of a team that is developing an access control system, manages working hours in countless options, and operates all over the country. I experienced several types of problems here, which contained debugging, writing automatic tests, doing manual tests, or developing new features.

After I finished my BSc, I started MSc in Data Science in an English course, although nowadays I prefer work experience to university since I feel it more useful considering my future. Moreover I can learn what I want - not what I have to - during my free time.

Since I spend most of my time in front of a computer, it is vital to have some training to reboot my mind and wake up my body to have energy for additional activities. I like using skipping ropes, go for a run, or skateboarding next to the Danube at night. Besides that, I have an addiction to sports and escaping rooms, therefore if you are looking for someone on this topic, I am the perfect candidate.
